The Lumineers

The Lumineers are an American folk rock band based in Denver, Colorado. The founding members are Wesley Schultz (lead vocalist, guitar) and Jeremiah Fraites (drums, percussion, piano). Schulz and Fraites began writing and performing together in Ramsey, New Jersey in 2005. Cellist and vocalist Neyla Pekarek (cello) joined the band in 2010, and was a member until 2018. They specialise in folk rock also termed as Americana genre of music. Their striped back raw sound is what endears them most to me. It is a very infectious sound with quite thoughtful lyrics.

Band Members:

Wesley Schultz

Wesley grew up in Ramsey, New Jersey, the son of Judy Schultz, and attended Ramsey High School and the University of Richmond. His best friend was Josh Fraites, who died of s drug overdose. In the wake of which he connected with Josh's younger brother, Jeremiah Fraites. The two found solace in music, writing songs and playing gigs around New York.

Jeremiah Caleb Fraites

He is an American musician, composer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist. He is the co-founder of The Lumineers and is a song-writer for the band. He was born and raised in Ramsey, New Jersey.
